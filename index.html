Lightning V3

Lightning V3 is our most human-sounding model yet. It goes beyond speed and clarity to capture how people really speak. With support for disfluencies like “uh” and “um,” instruction tuning for tone and style, and zero-shot voice cloning, it feels alive and expressive. You can guide it to speak calm, professional, playful, or excited, and it adapts instantly. With V3, you also gain the ability to tweak speech delivery in real time based on an users's response, making conversations more adaptive and context-sensitive. Compared to V2, which was built around fast and clear synthesis, Lightning V3 makes the leap to truly natural, human-like conversation.

All the demo examples are organized into three categories to highlight Lightning V3’s new capabilities:
– Conversations: Agent-to-agent dialogues that showcase natural back-and-forth flow, demonstrating how Lightning V3 can handle multi-turn interactions with context, tone shifts, and responsiveness.
– Instruction Tuning: The same sentence delivered in different ways — calm, excited, professional, or playful — based on natural language instructions.
– Disfluencies: Examples with natural pauses, “uh” and “you know,” showing how V3 handles real conversational flow.
– Varied Generation: From cloning a client’s voice for personalized marketing, to adapting tone for customer support, to instantly generating unique characters for games or audiobooks — showcasing how voices and styles can flex across real-world scenarios.
